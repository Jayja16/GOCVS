FAQs

Why did the GCE board create this verification system?
After due elaboration and consideration,the GCE board to provide her past candidates with such a platform to make the processing of their documents which include educational requirements easier.
Why is this service only available for certificates issued within the last 3 years?
Being a recent initiative and considering the amount of resources needed to put this project up, the GCE board decided to make it available for certificates involved within the last 3 years for management purposes and to analyse how it is received by the public. If the feedback is positive, we will see how to make it available to older candidates too
Verification of my certificate failed though I wrote the GCE exam. What do I do?
First, check if you have correctly entered your CIN as it is on your certificate. Secondly, if your certificate has not yet been issued then there will be no means of verifying it. If you keep experiencing the same problem, try refreshing the page and if the problem persists don't hesitate to contact us.
